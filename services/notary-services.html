Notary Services

Services / Notary Services

Our founding partner was, at the moment of his licensing examination, the youngest certified Federal Notary of the country. He is also, simultaneously, an active alternate State Notary.

As a federal notary public, we provide essential legal authentication services for a wide range of transactions. Our notary services ensure the legal validity and enforceability of your documents and transactions.

Our Notary Services Include:

  • Document authentication and certification
  • Power of attorney notarization
  • Corporate document authentication
  • Real estate transaction notarization
  • Apostille and consular legalization
  • Legal document translation and certification

Our notary services are conducted with the highest level of professionalism and attention to detail. We ensure all documents meet Mexican legal requirements and are properly executed for maximum legal protection.

Whether you need document authentication, contract notarization, or legal certification, our notary services provide the legal foundation and peace of mind you require for your transactions in Mexico.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican legal procedures, Lex Advisors has established strong relationships with reputable notaries throughout Baja California, ensuring efficient and reliable service for all your notarial needs.
