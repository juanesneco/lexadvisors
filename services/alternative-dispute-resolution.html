Services / Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)

At Lex Advisors, we are firm believers in non-adversarial approaches to conflict resolution. Disputes, if not handled properly, can escalate quicklyâ€”compromising agendas, reputations, and financial stability. Voluntary, mutually agreed-upon solutions are almost always more effective and sustainable than those imposed through litigation.

Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) methods often offer a faster, more cost-effective, and more satisfactory path to resolution than traditional legal proceedings. We provide personalized assessments and consultations to help you understand the scope, benefits, and limitations of ADR mechanisms such as mediation, conciliation, and arbitration.

When acting as mediators or conciliators, we do so with the highest level of fairness and professionalism, ensuring that relationships are preserved and that parties reach amicable, constructive agreements.

One of our partners is a certified mediator and is qualified to act as a federal arbitrator, bringing recognized expertise and credibility to the process.

We can provide the following services:

  • Coordination of mediation procedures
  • Drafting of arbitration clauses
  • Appointment of ADR facilitators
