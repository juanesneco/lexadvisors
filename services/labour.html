Employment Law

Services / Employment Law

Lex Advisors provides comprehensive labour law services to help both employers and employees navigate the complex Mexican employment legal framework.

Mexican labor law is complex and constantly evolving. Our labor law services help businesses navigate these complexities while protecting both employer and employee rights.

Our Labour Law Services Include:

  • Employment contract drafting and review
  • Workplace policy development
  • Employee handbook creation
  • Termination and severance matters
  • Labor dispute resolution
  • Union relations and collective bargaining
  • Workplace safety and health compliance

We help businesses create fair, compliant employment practices that protect both the company and its employees while minimizing legal risks.

Whether you're an employer establishing workplace policies, an employee facing workplace issues, or dealing with labour disputes, our labour law services provide the expert guidance and representation you need.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican labour law, Lex Advisors has helped hundreds of clients resolve employment matters, ensuring fair treatment, compliance, and successful outcomes.
