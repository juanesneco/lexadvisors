Construction Law

Services / Construction Law

Lex Advisors provides comprehensive construction and development legal services to ensure successful project execution and compliance throughout all phases of development.

Construction projects in Mexico involve complex legal requirements and potential risks. Our construction law services provide comprehensive legal support throughout the entire project lifecycle.

Our Construction Law Services Include:

  • Construction contract drafting and negotiation
  • Development agreement structuring
  • Permit and licensing assistance
  • Contractor and subcontractor agreements
  • Construction defect claims
  • Insurance and bonding matters
  • Project completion and warranty issues

We work closely with developers, contractors, and property owners to ensure projects proceed smoothly and are completed successfully.

Whether you're planning a development project, negotiating construction contracts, or dealing with construction disputes, our construction law services provide the legal foundation and strategic guidance you need for project success.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican construction law, Lex Advisors has helped hundreds of projects achieve successful completion, ensuring compliance, efficiency, and project success.
