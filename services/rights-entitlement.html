Rights & Entitlement

Services / Rights & Entitlement

Securing timely government permit approvals can be a complex challenge in any country—Mexico is no exception.

At Lex Advisors, our Land Use, Entitlements & Zoning group has an unmatched track record of efficiently obtaining the permits necessary to launch development projects across Baja California Sur. We don't just focus on securing approvals—we prioritize timing, knowing that delays can jeopardize an entire project. We also do not give a false sense of expectations and will tell the client with ethical transparency what is and is not feasible.

Our experience spans a wide range of projects, from simple re-zonings to complex permitting issues affecting both real estate and commercial land. We serve as strategic partners to our clients, offering legal insight that extends well beyond the close of a transaction.

Thanks to our long-standing relationships with key state, municipal, and federal officials, we provide clients with unrivaled access and a deep understanding of evolving policies and regulations. This insider perspective allows us to navigate bureaucratic processes with precision and speed—ensuring your project moves forward without unnecessary setbacks.

We firmly believe in doing things right. This is one of the differentiating factors that sets us apart.
