### Notary Services
**Short Description:** State and Federal notary services by Mexico's youngest certified Federal Notary, specializing in wills, trusts, and real estate transactions.

**Long Description:**
In Mexico, a Notary Public plays a vital role in the execution of many legal acts. Unlike in other jurisdictions, Mexican Notaries are highly qualified attorneys who have passed rigorous legal examinations and meet strict professional standards. Their involvement is essential in civil, business, and corporate matters—especially for foreign individuals and entities navigating the legal landscape.

Our founding partner was, at the time of his licensing examination, the **youngest certified Federal Notary in Mexico**. He currently serves as an active Alternate State Notary, bringing a unique blend of experience and distinction to our practice.

At Lex Advisors, we are fully equipped to provide **State and Federal Notary Services**, ensuring that your transactions are handled with precision, legality, and trust.

We maintain strong, trusted relationships with Notaries across the state of Baja California Sur, allowing us to guide you seamlessly through a wide range of notarial processes. Whether you're a local resident or a foreign investor, we ensure that your legal acts are executed with clarity, compliance, and efficiency.

Our Notary-related services include:
