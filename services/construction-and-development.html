Services / Construction and Development

Ideas and projects are exciting—but in the midst of creativity and enthusiasm, it's easy to overlook the critical details that ensure a project's success.

We specialize in entitlements, strategic planning, and procedural design for real estate developments, business ventures, and construction projects. Our expertise lies in building the right structure and logistics for development teams to operate efficiently, integrating professionals from diverse disciplines, backgrounds, and even countries. This multidisciplinary approach ensures a comprehensive perspective that delivers results—streamlining licensing, preventing legal issues and reducing costs.

Our holistic coordination spans every phase of a real estate development:

  • Preliminary project planning
  • Licensing and permitting
  • Contractor oversight
  • Condominium/subdivision structuring
  • Closing of the properties

All of this is done with a clear focus on the original vision and end-goal of the project.

We bring distinctive experience in setting up legal frameworks for complex developments, including condominiums, high-end culinary centers, restaurants, and boutique hotels. With Lex Advisors, your project is not only well-structured—it's strategically positioned for success.
