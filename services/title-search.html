Title Search

Services / Title Search

Lex Advisors provides comprehensive title search services to help clients minimize liabilities and secure title insurance for their real estate investments.

Title search services are essential for any real estate transaction in Mexico. Our comprehensive title verification process ensures clear ownership and identifies potential legal issues before they become problems.

Our Title Search Services Include:

  • Chain of title verification
  • Lien and encumbrance searches
  • Property boundary and survey analysis
  • Utility and easement documentation
  • Government permit verification
  • Foreign investment compliance checks
  • Title insurance coordination

We provide detailed title reports with recommendations for resolving any identified issues. Our thorough process protects our clients from costly legal problems and ensures smooth property transactions.

Whether you're purchasing property, securing financing, or conducting investment due diligence, our title search services provide the comprehensive analysis and protection you need for secure real estate transactions.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican real estate law, Lex Advisors has conducted thousands of title searches throughout Baja California, ensuring secure transactions and comprehensive protection for our clients.
