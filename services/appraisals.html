Services / Appraisals

Assessing the value of an asset is one thing—understanding the implications of assigning that value is another. At Lex Advisors, we excel at both. Our deep understanding of valuation and its consequences allows us to guide you through critical transactions with insight, integrity, and strategic clarity.

While real estate remains our primary focus in the appraisal field, our expertise extends to the valuation of businesses, corporations, legal entities, goods, and other commercial assets. We are recognized professionals in these areas, trusted for our precision and ethical approach.

Our team includes nationally accredited appraisers whose work is respected not only across Mexico but also on the international stage. With Lex Advisors, you receive valuations that are not only technically sound but also strategically informed—empowering you to make confident decisions.

  • Fiscal/Tax Appraisals
  • Commercial Appraisals
  • Business Appraisal (Actualización de Rentas)
