Corporate Services

Services / Corporate Services

We have experience representing clients in some of the most complex and high-impact corporate processes in the history of Baja California Sur.

As a complement to our broader legal and strategic services, we offer comprehensive support in the creation, transformation, dissolution, and liquidation of legal entities. We also assist with the drafting and formalization of shareholder meetings and other advanced corporate actions. Our team is well-versed in handling major transactions, including mergers and acquisitions, and provides thorough legal due diligence to ensure every detail is accounted for.

In a constantly evolving legal landscape, staying compliant is not optional—it's essential. We help our clients understand how to meet legal requirements while protecting key business interests, mitigating risks, and maintaining operational flexibility.

Our corporate services include:

  • Draft of General Assemblies
  • Mergers & Acquisitions
  • Governance and Management Structures
  • Shareholder Conflict Resolution
  • Due Diligence of Corporations
  • Creation, Transformation and Liquidation of entities
  • Corporate Compliance
  • Secretarial Advisory Services

With Lex Advisors, your corporate structure is not only legally sound—it's strategically aligned with your business goals.
