Services / Corporate Services

Lex Advisors provides comprehensive corporate legal services to help businesses establish, operate, and grow successfully in Mexico.

Our corporate legal services support businesses of all sizes, from startups to multinational corporations. We provide comprehensive legal guidance to help your business operate successfully in Mexico.

Our Corporate Services Include:

  • Business formation and registration
  • Corporate governance and compliance
  • Contract drafting and negotiation
  • Intellectual property protection
  • Mergers and acquisitions
  • Corporate restructuring
  • Regulatory compliance and licensing

We understand the complexities of doing business in Mexico and provide practical, cost-effective solutions that protect your interests while ensuring compliance with Mexican law.

Whether you're starting a new business, expanding operations, or restructuring existing entities, our corporate services provide the legal foundation and strategic guidance you need for success in the Mexican market.

With 25+ years of experience in Mexican corporate law, Lex Advisors has helped hundreds of businesses establish and maintain their corporate presence in Mexico, ensuring compliance, efficiency, and long-term success.
